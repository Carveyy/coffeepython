Our Story

In 1964, the first Digital Espresso restaurant in Hamilton, Ontario opened its doors and Canadians have been ordering Digital Espresso's iconic Original Blend coffee, Double-Double ™ coffees, Donuts and Espresso bits® ever since. Over the years, Digital Espresso has captured the hearts and taste buds of Canadians. Digital Espresso is now proud to be Canada's largest restaurant chain serving over 5 million cups of coffee every day with 80% of Canadians visiting a store in Canada at least once a month.
